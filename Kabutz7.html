Rachel Hearst Reflects on Her Journey as Kachoo’s Brand Ambassador

Brand Ambassador for Kazine Volume 1 Issue 1 & Issue 2 reflects on collaboration, storytelling, and meaningful impact.

March 2026
Jakarta
#KachooFamily

In an exclusive interview with the Kaboom team, Rachel Theodora Hearst — Brand Ambassador for Kazine Volume 1 Issue 1 and Issue 2 — shared her experience of representing Kachoo over the past two editions. She described the journey as not only memorable but deeply meaningful for her creative career.

"I'm grateful to be part of something that connects people through ideas and visuals."
Rachel Hearst

When asked how it felt to represent Kachoo, Rachel said she felt incredibly grateful for the opportunity. According to her, the role allowed her to be part of a platform that truly values creativity and the power of storytelling. For Rachel, Kachoo is more than just a media platform — it is a space where ideas, visuals, and stories come together to reach and inspire a wider audience.

Reflecting on her journey with Kachoo in the Kazine Project, Rachel noted that the most memorable part for her was the collaborative process with the creative team. She explained that every project felt like a shared vision, where each member contributed their own ideas, energy, and perspective. The experience made her realize how powerful teamwork can be in creating something truly impactful.

Rachel also added that one of the things she enjoyed the most while working with the Kachoo team was the positive and supportive environment. She felt that everyone involved shared the same passion for what they were doing. That energy, she said, made the entire process feel like more than just work — it became a creative journey that everyone experienced together to produce something meaningful.

A new perspective on the creative industry

Her experience as a Brand Ambassador also gave Rachel a new perspective on her personal journey in the creative industry. She realized that being part of this industry is not only about appearance, but also about the stories that are told and the people one meets along the way. It is a lesson she believes will continue to shape her as her career grows in the future.

"My time with Kachoo has been a beautiful journey of creativity, collaboration, and meaningful connections."
Rachel Hearst

Closing the interview, Rachel summarized her time with Kachoo in a simple yet meaningful way. For her, being a Brand Ambassador has been a beautiful journey filled with creativity, collaboration, and meaningful connections.
